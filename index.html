ABOUT


Culling Game is a never ending game designed by Kenjaku to stores lots of cursed energy from the players in the name of envolving humanity. Everyone who involved in Jujutsu are required to face this game. Players must adhere to the strict rules of the game and have a fight between jujutsu users, that serve as players.

GUIDES




RULES

  1. One account per head. We don't accept any twins unless the desired character have one.
  2. We are accepting only 2D characters (anime, manga)
  3. Interactions with outsider is strictly not allowed.
  4. Please be active as we will be running for 7 days only. Please inform the base if you're going to be inactive for awhile. Maximum day for hiatus is 1 day.
  5. You need to reach 50 tweets upon your arrival and 30 tweets daily. Tweet such as greeting (good morning, good afternoon, etc) and no interaction will not be count.
  6. Changing character will be allowed 48 hours later after verification and please note that you can only change your character once. Please consider it wisely.
  7. Keep the timeline PG-13. Profanities are allowed but we hope you'll lessen it.
  8. Keep your identity secret. Exchanging anything that will reveal your identity (cyber acc, plato, etc) is prohibited until we're closed.
  9. Please be kind and respect everyone.
  10. Characters from Jujutsu Kaisen who are deceased and the object of Culling Games are STRICTLY prohibited. Ex: Geto Suguru, Gojo Satoru, Nanami Kento.

HOW TO JOIN

  1. Make sure to clean your following list from the account that you'll be using and follow our main base only (@SHlMETSUKAIYU).
  2. Read and understand each point of the rules before you decide to proceed to the next step.
  3. Fill in the form that we will provide once we've released it.
  4. If you're accepted, you can change your username with format: @GRADE(NUMBER)(NAME)
    5. Ex: @GRADE01NOBARA (all in capital) please do not change your username before you're accepted.
